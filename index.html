ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

I am currently a second year student studying Computer Science with Web Development at the University of Salford. I have always had a passion for technology from a young age. Technology is capable of impacting millions of people, the idea that I can impact someone else anywhere in the world is what drives my passion for Tech. I also currenlty run an instagram page based on technology with over 30k followers, I have created my own little community with people all over the world with the same passion as my self.

Before coming to the United Kindom, I had already lived in 2 other countries. Born in Zimbabwe, spent half of my childhood in the beautiful lands of South Africa and the other half in Dortmund, Germany. I then graduated from secondary school and college in Manchester, UK and now a University student.

What do I do ?

I mainly focus on building Web Applications from Front-End using common front end technologies such as React , HTML/CSS and JavaScript to Back-End using mostly PHP for University projects and Node.JS for my own personal work. I also use frameworks like Laravel, BootStrap(including preprocessor SASS) and Express to help minimise development time and complexity. Besides the web, I have also learnt some Object-Oriented Langauges from self teaching through online resources in my spare time or University modules. I have written small desktop applications in JAVA and C#.
