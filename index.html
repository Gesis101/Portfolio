ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

I am currently a second year student studying Computer Science with Web Development at the University of Salford. I have always had a passion for technology from a young age. One of my first experiences with computers; in 9th grade, I wrote a simple yet effective program that helped with my maths homework

Before coming to the United Kindom, I had already lived in 2 other countries. Born in Zimbabwe, spent half of my childhood in the beautiful lands of South Africa and the other half in Dortmund, Germany. I then graduated from secondary school and college in Manchester, UK and now a University student.

What do I do ?

I mainly focus on building Web Applications from Front-End using common front end technologies such as React , HTML/CSS and JavaScript to Back-End using mostly PHP for University projects and Node.JS for my own personal work. I also use frameworks like Laravel, BootStrap(including preprocessor SASS) and Express to help minimise development time and complexity.

Besides the web, I have also learnt some Object-Oriented Langauges from self teaching through online resources in my spare time or University modules. I have written small desktop applications in JAVA and C#.
